MODEL SCOPE

What this estimate means

The planner searches a configurable time-of-flight and arrival-offset grid, solves the heliocentric two-body Lambert problem, and ranks solutions by departure plus arrival velocity mismatch.

Planetary perturbations, finite burns, launch windows, navigation uncertainty, and asteroid capture dynamics are outside this model.
